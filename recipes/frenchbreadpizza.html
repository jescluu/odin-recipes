French Bread Pizza Recipe

Description

  • French Bread
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Oregano
  • San Marazano Tomatoes
  • Pepperoni
  • Shredded mozarella cheese
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. In a pan, heat butter, garlic, oregano
  2. Once cooked, use about half of the garlic oil and put on French bread
  3. Add mozarella cheese to French Bread, then heat in oven for 7 minutes
  4. Add tomato sauce to the remaining garlic oil in a pan
  5. Remove French Bread from oven and add tomato sauce to French Bread
  6. Add an additional layer of mozarella cheese
  7. Layer with pepperoni and sliced mushrooms
  8. Place pizza back into oven for an additional 10 minutes
  9. Enjoy that delicious pizza
