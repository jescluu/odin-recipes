Penne Vodka Pasta Recipe

Description

  • Penne Pasta
  • San Marazano Tomatoes (Canned)
  • Vodka
  • Prosciutto
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut garlic into thin slices and head over medium on oiled pan.
  2. Cut prosciutto into bite size pieces and cook with garlic
  3. After 3-4 minutes, add San Marazano Tomatoes and crush until smooth, add basil
  4. Cook for additional 5 minutes, then remove basil and add Vodka
  5. Cook off Vodka
  6. Reduce sauce until thickened, about 30-40 minutes.
  7. Boil Penne Pasta
  8. Add Heavy Cream
  9. Mix well and reduce once again
  10. Add cooked Penne Pasta and enjoy!
