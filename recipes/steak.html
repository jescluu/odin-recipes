Ribeye Steak Recipe

Description

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Season steak generously with salt and pepper
  2. Mince garlic or keep as cloves
  3. Make sure your pan is hot and oiled
  4. Place steak onto pan and make sure it is sizzling to provide a nice sear
  5. Add garlic and basil
  6. After 4-5 minutes, flip and baste with butter
  7. Cook until internal temp of 130 for medium rare
  8. Remove from pan, let rest for 10 minutes
  9. Enjoy that delicious piece of meat
